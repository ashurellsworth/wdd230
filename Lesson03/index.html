Kalamazoo Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

About the Site

Purpose

The Kalamazoo Chamber of Commerce aims to help the city of Kalamazoo thrive and grow by strengthening the bonds of its community. Our desire is to impulse the business owners and entrepreneurs that reside within our borders to in turn make Kalamazoo strong and better every day.

Scenarios

  • How can I as a business owner become part of the Kalamazoo Chamber of Commerce?
  • Where can I learn more about the history of this organization?
  • Who are the leaders of the chamber?
